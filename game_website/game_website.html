* DOCKING: RMB
* SAT RECOND: Shift

MINING:

As an employee of Sol Mining Co. your sole purpose in life is getting us as many asteroids as possible.
Keep the asteroids rolling in and life will be good for you.
Solar System 223 asteroids are high in carbon, iron, titanium, aluminum, and vanadium.

MINING PROCEDURE:

1: IDENTIFY an asteroid using your HUD.
2: DOCK (RMB) with the asteroid.
3: MINE the asteroid.
4: UNDOCK with (RMB).
