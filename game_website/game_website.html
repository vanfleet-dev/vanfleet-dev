Open Source 2D Rougelike Space Shooter

Welcome to SPACE SHOOTER.

You have been assinged to solar system 223 to fulfill your contractual obligations with Sol Mining Co.
We have detected signifigant drone activity in the area.
This could cause some unexpected interuptions to your mining operations.
As you know death does not free you from your contractual obligations.
Sol Mining Co. is proud to provide our employees industry standard stack re-compiles, body replacements and ship lease policies for when the drones inevitably turn you into dust.*
Suit up, its time to fly.

* Sol Mining Co. holds no resposibility for lost ship upgrades at the time of death.

SHIP SPECS:

The company issue ship for this solar system is a dual purpose fighter and miner.
CONTROLS:
* Travel mode: A and D rotate, W and S thrusters.
* Precision mode: Hold SpaceBar , press A , D , W , S for thrusters, ship follows cursor.
* Shoot: LMB Shooting activates precision mode.
* Docking: RMB
* Map: Shift
